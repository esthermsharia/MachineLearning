Analysis of correlation between churning and other attributes Based on correlation matrix¶

The correlation coefficient ranges from 1 to 1. When it is close to 1, it means there is a strong positive correlation; the correlation matrix gives us the following insight The possibility of an employee churning is highly related to the time spend in the company, the more The time spend in the company the higher the chances of churning. The less satified the employee is the more likely he/she is to churn. Employees with work accidents are likely to churn. Average monthly hours spent in the company has negligible effect on the attrition of employees. The value of the last evaluation had minimal effect on attrition.
